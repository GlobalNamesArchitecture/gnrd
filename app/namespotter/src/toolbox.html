    Name-finding engine
    TaxonFinder is a dictionary-based engine. It is more accurate when names are known. NetiNeti is a language processing engine that may discover more names, but can result in false positives.
    About
    NameSpotter was developed by David P. Shorthouse at the Center for Library and Informatics, Marine Biological Laboratory for the Global Names project. The name-finding engines, TaxonFinder and NetiNeti were developed by his colleagues.
